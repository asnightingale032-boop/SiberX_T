Cyberattack Disrupts SiberX Transit Signage, PA and App Across the Network

By ODTN Newsroom · Updated · watching live

Commuters across the SiberX Transit Systems network reported false "evacuate" messages on station signage and public address systems this morning, alongside a wave of suspicious mobile app notifications, in what the operator has confirmed is an active cybersecurity incident.

SiberX says its security operations center detected the anomaly through conflicting operational data between station systems and central control, and has since isolated affected services. The operator says no fare payment data is confirmed compromised at this time and that an investigation, including the possibility of insider involvement, is underway.

ODTN will continue to update this story as the situation develops. Riders are urged to rely only on official SiberX channels for service information.
